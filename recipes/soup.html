Soup

Description

Vegtable soup is one of the best meals for a cool winter day!

it's simple to make and very tasty.

Ingredients

  • water
  • canned tomatoes
  • green beans
  • pasta
  • beans
  • corn

Steps

  1. Combine water, tomatoes,green beans and corn in slowcooker.
  2. Turn slowcooker to high.
  3. Cook until ingredients are tender (6-8hrs).
  4. Once tender mix in pasta for last hour.
  5. Serve hot, enjoy!
